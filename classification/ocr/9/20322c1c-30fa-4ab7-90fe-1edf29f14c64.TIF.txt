Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)

(0) _ EPA Schedules Meeting of Scientific Counsclors

EPA has scheduled a meeting of the Executive Committee of the Board of Scientific Counselors
for August 17-18, 1998, in Arlington, Virginia. According to the meeting announcement,
“Agenda items will include, but not be limited to: State of ORD [Office of Research and
Development), Activities of the Science Advisory Board, and Issues Definition on Particulate
Matter.” For additional meeting information, contact Shirley Hamilton at (202) 564-6853. See
Federal Register, July 30, 1998.
